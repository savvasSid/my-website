My Journey In Sports

Welcome to the sports corner of my personal website! Throughout my athletic career, I've had the privilege of participating in various sports, each contributing to my growth and character.

I dedicated the biggest part of my sports career to football, which lasted 15 years with many successes and failures in equal measure. I had several prospects to enter this sport, although I did not manage to play professionally. Competing in A local for 4 years and in C category for 1 year. After that, I had been involved in kick boxing for a few months but I didn't like it very much as it was too much for me and I left it relatively quickly. Finally, as my student life began I stopped football due to lack of time and started working with the gymnasium where I still work today. I also participate in a student soccer team where we compete in a student tournament, as well as many other sports activities related to female students, such as hiking, hiking, etc.

Whatever you do in life, never leave sports behind.
